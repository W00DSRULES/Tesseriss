Privacy Policy

Tesseriss · published by Vibalyze OÜ

Last updated: 31 May 2026

The short version: Tesseriss collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no network connection. Everything stays on your device.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Tesseriss has no user accounts, no sign-in, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking of any kind. The app does not make network requests and works fully offline.

Data stored on your device

Your high scores and preferences (such as game mode, theme, language, and sound settings) are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is permanently removed when you delete the app.

Third parties

Tesseriss includes no third-party software development kits, advertising networks, or analytics services. No data is shared with anyone because none is collected.

Children's privacy

Tesseriss is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
